## Day 3 Summary – Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Today, I performed comprehensive Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the mutual fund dataset using Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn, and Plotly.

### Key Findings

* Mutual fund NAVs showed a strong long-term upward trend.
* SBI Mutual Fund maintained the highest AUM among fund houses.
* SIP inflows reached an all-time high of ₹31,002 crore in December 2025.
* Investors aged 26–35 formed the largest investor segment.
* Equity-oriented investments witnessed stronger growth compared to debt and hybrid categories.
* Folio counts nearly doubled during the analysis period, indicating increasing retail participation.
